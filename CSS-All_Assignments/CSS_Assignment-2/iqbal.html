Allama Muhammad IQBAL


To A Young Man Your sofas are from Europe, your carpets from Iran,
My eyes weep blood when I see the youth in such luxury and ease.
What good is it if you possess the splendor of Chosroes' kingdom,
If you do not possess the bravery of Ali or the contentment of Sulaymaan.
Do not look for this in the glitter of today's civilization,
You will find this contentment in the peak of Imaan. When an eagle's spirit awakens in the youth,
It sees its destination in the heavens. Despair not, for despair brings about the decline of knowledge and soul:
The hope of a believer is among the confidants of God.
Your dwelling is not on the dome of a royal palace, You are an eagle, make your dwelling on the highest of mountains.
~~ Allama Muhammad Iqbal ~~
